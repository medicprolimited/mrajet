About This Tool

The Vaping Misinformation Detector helps you identify misleading or false claims about vaping in online content. By analyzing web articles and comparing claims against scientific evidence, this tool provides fact-based rebuttals to common misconceptions about vaping safety and health effects.

Why This Matters

Misinformation about vaping can lead to dangerous health decisions. Whether claims minimize risks ("vaping is completely harmless") or exaggerate them beyond the evidence, accurate information is essential for public health and personal decision-making.

How It Works

1. Input: Paste the URL of an article about vaping you want to analyze
2. Analysis: Our system extracts and analyzes the text content
3. Detection: The tool identifies specific claims that contradict current scientific consensus
4. Evidence: For each identified claim, we provide factual corrections with scientific sources
5. Report: Receive a summary of findings with citations to reliable research
